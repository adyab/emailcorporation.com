Our Services

The Email Corporation offers a range of email-adjacent services. The following represent a partial list.

■

Address Lifecycle Stewardship

We create, maintain, and retire email addresses on behalf of clients. Addresses are monitored throughout their operational lifespan. Retirement is handled discreetly.

■

Organizational Signal Management

Incoming communications are received, assessed, and routed to appropriate destinations within your organization. Or held. Depending on configuration.

■

Identity Distance Services

For clients who prefer that certain correspondents not have direct knowledge of their primary address. The mechanism by which this is accomplished is not described here.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of email-adjacent solutions designed to meet the needs of organizations with email requirements.

Email Presence Management

Establish and maintain a credible email presence across all relevant domains and sub-domains. Our proprietary PresenceCore™ technology ensures your email addresses remain present.

Address Provisioning

We provision email addresses. The addresses are then provisioned. Following provisioning, the addresses are available for use by the provisioned party or parties.

Continuity Assurance

Industry-leading uptime metrics ensure that your email infrastructure continues to function in a manner consistent with continued functioning. Results may vary.

Domain Integration

Seamlessly integrate email services with your existing domain infrastructure. Our DomainBridge™ platform facilitates the integration of email into domain-related contexts.

Compliance & Governance

Navigate the complex regulatory landscape of email with confidence. Our team is aware of most regulations and tracks developments on a semi-regular basis.

Strategic Consulting

Engage our consultants to discuss your email needs in a structured, billable setting. Consultants will listen and respond using words associated with email.

About The Email Corporation

The Email Corporation was established to address the email needs of organizations that had such needs. Since that time, we have continued to address those needs in a manner consistent with our founding purpose.

Our team is composed of professionals with backgrounds in email and related areas. Many of our staff have worked with email for a number of years. Some have received recognition for this work, internally.

We are headquartered in a location with reliable internet access. Our infrastructure is distributed across multiple facilities, each of which is capable of supporting email operations under normal and most abnormal conditions.
